Where the forest meets the shore.

Experience the raw beauty of the Latvian coast in our architect-designed retreat. Tucked away within a whisper of the Baltic tide, Pine Coast House offers a sanctuary where modern minimalism meets the rustic warmth of the Nordic forest.

Our house is crafted from sustainable local timber, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows that invite the changing light of the seasons directly into your living space. Cozy up by the fireplace or listen to the rhythmic pulse of the sea from your private terrace.
