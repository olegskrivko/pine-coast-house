Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your stay at Pine Coast House.

Where is Pine Coast House located?

Pine Coast House is located in Staldzene, near Ventspils on Latvia’s western Baltic Sea coast. It is surrounded by pine forest, dunes, and quiet coastal nature.

How far is the beach?

The Baltic Sea beach is within walking distance through pine forest and dunes, typically a 5–10 minute walk. The nearby Ventspils coastline includes Blue Flag beaches, a certification awarded for excellent water quality, safety, and environmental standards.

Does Pine Coast House have a sauna?

Yes — Pine Coast House includes a private Nordic-style sauna designed for relaxing after beach walks, forest hikes, and colder Baltic evenings. The sauna offers a peaceful wellness experience surrounded by pine forest and coastal nature.

Where can I buy groceries and essentials?

The nearest grocery stores, supermarkets, and pharmacies are located in Ventspils, approximately 15–20 minutes by car. The house is also equipped with basic long-stay essentials and kitchen supplies for comfortable stays.

How far is it from Riga?

Pine Coast House is approximately a 3-hour scenic drive from Riga by car, heading toward Latvia’s western coast near Ventspils.

Is the area private and quiet?

Yes — Pine Coast House is located in a quiet pine forest area with the nearest neighbors approximately 500 metres away. It offers a high level of privacy, and guests are rarely disturbed. The surrounding nature is peaceful and secluded. The nearby beach area is public, so it is possible that other visitors may walk along the shoreline, especially during the day.

What can I do nearby?

Nearby activities include exploring Staldzenes Stāvkrasts coastal cliffs, walking or cycling around Būšnieku Lake, visiting birdwatching towers, SUP and boat rentals, and seasonal stops like House of Lavender Ventspils. Guests also enjoy forest walks, quiet beaches, and Baltic Sea sunsets.

How many guests can stay?

Up to 6 guests can stay comfortably at Pine Coast House.

Are pets allowed?

Yes — pets are welcome at Pine Coast House. We provide basic pet-friendly amenities, including sleeping areas for dogs and toys for comfort. For cats, there is a dedicated indoor climbing and resting space designed for enrichment and relaxation.

What is the best type of stay?

Pine Coast House is made for slow mornings, long coastal walks, and evenings filled with golden light over the sea. It’s a place to reconnect with nature — whether you’re watching sunsets through pine trees, taking scenic photos along the dunes, or simply enjoying the quiet rhythm of the forest. Perfect for romantic getaways, peaceful family stays, or anyone looking to slow down and breathe deeply by the Baltic coast.
